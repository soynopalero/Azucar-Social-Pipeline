Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Out And About (operating as Azúcar at Out & About), located at 327 W Lewis St, Pasco, WA, USA ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website, our Facebook Page, our Instagram account, and the Meta application "Azucar Posters" used to publish content to our own social media channels.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from members of the public through the Azucar Posters application. The app is used solely by authorized administrators of Out And About to publish marketing content (event flyers, photos, videos, and announcements) to our own Facebook Page and Instagram account via the Meta Graph API.

The information processed by the application is limited to:

  • Authentication tokens issued by Meta to our authorized administrators.
  • Page and account identifiers for our own Facebook Page and Instagram Business account.
  • Media content (images, videos, text captions) created by us for publication.

2. How We Use Information

The information described above is used only to:

  • Publish posts, photos, and videos to our own Facebook Page and Instagram account.
  • Schedule posts for future publication.
  • Manage our own social media presence as a small business.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share personal information with third parties. The only third party that processes data through this application is Meta Platforms, Inc. (Facebook and Instagram), through their official Graph API, in order to publish content to our own accounts.

4. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens and credentials are stored locally in environment configuration files on devices operated by authorized administrators of Out And About. We take reasonable measures to protect this information from unauthorized access, including restricted file permissions and excluding credentials from any public code repositories.

5. Public Content

Posts published to our Facebook Page (facebook.com/outandaboutpasco) and Instagram account (@cluboutandabout) are public by their nature and are governed by Meta's own Privacy Policy and Instagram's Data Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Out And About is a 21+ venue. Our content and digital channels are not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data from the public through the Azucar Posters application, there is no personal data for individuals to access, correct, or delete. If you interact with our Facebook Page or Instagram account directly (for example by commenting, messaging, or following), your data is handled by Meta in accordance with their policies.

If you wish to request deletion of any data Meta may have shared with us, please contact us at the address below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of our digital properties after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
